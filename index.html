5500 a typeface experiment


New technology makes it interesting to think about how typography
will change in the future. The letters created for this project are unfamiliar
and unreadable for todays people as human language will change aswell. At the starting point of the project a study began to examine japanese letters from 17th century. Some of the letters of the 17th century are unreadable in present time which brought ideas about the evolution of future typography.

1

2 3 4 During the project questions came up in what directions the alphabet can be read in the future. In Virtual reality it can be possible to read alphabets in a completely new way than we are used to. The future of typography may also be affected by gravity which can adjust the shape and movement.
